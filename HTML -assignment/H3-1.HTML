Kim Tae-hyung (Korean: 김태형; born December 30, 1995),[1] also known professionally as V, is a South Korean singer and songwriter. A member of the South Korean boy band BTS,[2] V has released three solo tracks under the band's name: "Stigma" in 2016, "Singularity" in 2018, and "Inner Child" in 2020, all of which have charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart. In 2019, V released his first independent song, the self-composed digital track "Scenery". He also appeared on the Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth (2016) soundtrack alongside bandmate Jin.
